BLACKPINK's Jennie Named to Time 100 Most Influential People List

April 16, 2026 • 12:15 PM KST By Julian Choi 3 min read

BLACKPINK member Jennie has been listed among U.S. Time magazine's "100 Most Influential People of the Year," becoming the only Korean included in the prestigious annual list for 2026.

The singer was named in the Artists category of the Time 100 announced Wednesday (U.S. time), joining global figures from politics, entertainment, science, and business who have made significant impact over the past year.

Historic Achievement for K-Pop

Jennie's inclusion marks a historic moment for K-pop, representing the genre's growing global influence beyond music charts and into broader cultural impact. Time's recognition highlights how Korean pop culture has become a significant force in shaping global trends and conversations.

"Jennie represents the new face of global influence," wrote Time in its profile. "Through music, fashion, and cultural leadership, she has helped redefine what it means to be a global superstar in the digital age."

Career Milestones

The BLACKPINK member has had a remarkable year, with her solo activities gaining international attention alongside the group's continued success. Her fashion collaborations, solo music releases, and global brand ambassador roles have positioned her as one of the most recognizable faces in entertainment worldwide.

Industry analysts note that Jennie's influence extends beyond traditional metrics, with her social media presence and fashion choices often sparking global trends and discussions about Asian representation in global media.

Reaction from the Industry

Fellow artists and industry leaders have congratulated Jennie on the achievement. BLACKPINK's agency, YG Entertainment, released a statement expressing pride in the recognition and highlighting the group's ongoing commitment to positive global impact.

Fans worldwide have celebrated the news on social media, with #JennieTime100 trending across multiple platforms. Many have noted the significance of a K-pop artist being recognized alongside world leaders, scientists, and business innovators.

What's Next

With this recognition, expectations are high for Jennie's future projects. Sources close to the artist suggest new music and creative ventures are in development, though no official announcements have been made.

The Time 100 gala is scheduled for later this month in New York City, where honorees will be celebrated at an exclusive event attended by global leaders and celebrities.
