IVE Yujin's Alleged Dating Rumors With Actor Moon Sangmin Triggers Massive Reactions

March 17, 2026 By Selena Jung 📖 9 min read

The K-pop and K-drama worlds have collided in spectacular fashion as dating rumors linking IVE's leader Yujin with rising actor Moon Sangmin have exploded across social media, triggering massive and deeply divided reactions from fans of both celebrities. The alleged relationship, if true, would unite two of Korea's most promising young stars in what many are already calling a potential "power couple."

The rumors began circulating after eagle-eyed fans noticed what they believe are multiple pieces of "evidence" suggesting a romantic connection between the two stars. Social media detectives have compiled photos showing the pair allegedly wearing matching accessories, posting suspiciously similar Instagram content around the same times, and reportedly being spotted in the same neighborhood on multiple occasions.

While neither party has confirmed or denied the relationship, the rumors have taken on a life of their own, dominating online discussions and trending across multiple platforms. The intensity of the response reflects both stars' significant fanbases and the enduring fascination with celebrity relationships in Korean pop culture.

The "Evidence" Fueling Speculation

Those convinced of the relationship have compiled an extensive dossier of supposed evidence. The most-cited piece involves what fans claim is a matching phone case spotted in separate photos of each celebrity. While the case design is relatively common, believers argue that the timing and style are too coincidental to dismiss.

Additionally, internet sleuths have noted that both Yujin and Moon Sangmin posted sunset photos on the same evening that appear to be taken from similar vantage points. While not definitive proof of being together, fans have geolocated the images to the same Seoul neighborhood, fueling speculation that the two were together.

Perhaps most compelling to believers are reports of multiple sightings of the pair in the same locations. One anonymous tip, shared on a popular forum, claims a restaurant employee saw them dining together in a private room at an upscale Korean barbecue restaurant known for its celebrity clientele. Another report suggests they were seen entering the same apartment building on different occasions.

"When you put all the evidence together, it's hard to deny there's something going on. The matching items, the similar posts, the location sightings—it's too much to be coincidence. I think they're definitely dating but keeping it private." — Comment from a popular fan investigation account

Fan Reactions: A House Divided

The response from IVE's fandom, known as DIVE, has been notably mixed. A significant portion of fans have expressed support for Yujin's potential happiness, adopting the increasingly common position among younger K-pop fans that idols deserve private lives and romantic relationships.

"Yujin is 21 years old. She's allowed to date," wrote one supportive fan. "If she and Moon Sangmin make each other happy, we should be happy for them. This obsessive need to keep idols single and 'available' is unhealthy and we need to move past it as a fandom."

However, other fans have expressed disappointment, betrayal, or outright anger at the rumors. Some have announced they're "dropping" Yujin as their bias or withdrawing support from IVE entirely. This subset of fans argues that as a relatively new group still building their career, dating scandals could damage IVE's momentum.

"I'm not angry that she might be dating—I'm angry that she might be dating during such a crucial time for the group," wrote one conflicted fan. "IVE is on the rise. Why risk it all now? Can't she wait a few more years?"

Moon Sangmin's Fanbase Responds

The reaction from Moon Sangmin's fans has been similarly divided, though the general tone appears somewhat more supportive. Actor fandoms in Korea tend to be more accepting of dating news compared to idol fandoms, partly because the relationship expectations differ between the two celebrity categories.

Moon Sangmin, who rose to prominence through his breakout role in a hit 2025 drama, has cultivated a romantic leading man image that some fans argue is actually enhanced by having a real-life relationship. "It makes him more romantic, more desirable," argued one fan.

Still, some fans have expressed disappointment, particularly those who harbored hopes of dating the actor themselves or who felt invested in his "available" bachelor image. The intensity of these feelings reflects the parasocial relationships that develop between celebrities and their devoted followers.

Agency Responses

Starship Entertainment, which manages IVE, released a characteristically vague statement in response to media inquiries. "We cannot confirm details of our artists' private lives," the statement read. "We ask for understanding regarding matters that are personal to our artists."

This non-denial denial has been interpreted differently by various parties. Some see it as implicit confirmation that there is indeed a relationship but that the agency doesn't want to officially acknowledge it. Others argue that it's standard agency language used to deflect any private life inquiry regardless of accuracy.

Moon Sangmin's agency released a similarly ambiguous statement, neither confirming nor denying the relationship and asking for respect for the actor's privacy. This coordinated non-response has only fueled further speculation, with some arguing that if the rumors were completely baseless, the agencies would simply deny them outright.

The "Shipping" Phenomenon

Interestingly, a subset of fans from both fandoms have embraced the potential pairing, creating fan content celebrating the rumored relationship. These "shippers" have given the couple a portmanteau name and begun producing fan art, edits, and speculative fiction about their romance.

"They look so good together," enthused one shipper account. "Both are tall, gorgeous, successful young people at the top of their fields. If they're actually dating, it's literally like a drama come to life. I'm fully on board."

This phenomenon reflects the complex and sometimes contradictory nature of fan culture, where the same fanbase can contain both those outraged by a relationship and those celebrating it. The shipping community has become a vocal presence in the discussion, pushing back against negativity with aggressive positivity.

Yujin's Position In IVE

As IVE's leader and one of its most popular members, Yujin holds a particularly visible position that magnifies the impact of any personal news. The 21-year-old, who previously gained fame as a member of IZ*ONE, has been instrumental in IVE's meteoric rise since their 2021 debut.

Yujin's image has generally emphasized maturity, leadership, and girl-crush appeal rather than the "girlfriend" concept that makes some fandoms particularly sensitive to dating news. This may partially explain why a significant portion of DIVE has been relatively accepting of the rumors.

However, IVE's concept as a whole emphasizes a certain aspirational, almost unattainable glamour that could be affected by "humanizing" details like dating. Industry observers are watching closely to see whether the rumors impact IVE's upcoming activities or commercial performance.

Impact On Upcoming Schedules

Both Yujin and Moon Sangmin have busy schedules ahead, with IVE preparing for a major comeback and Moon Sangmin filming a new drama. The timing of these rumors—whether accidental or deliberate—adds additional pressure to already demanding workloads.

Some industry observers have noted that dating rumors, while potentially disruptive in the short term, can actually boost public interest in upcoming projects. "Any publicity is publicity," noted one entertainment reporter. "If handled correctly, this could actually draw more attention to both of their upcoming works."

However, the risk of fan backlash affecting sales or ratings remains a concern for both management teams. Navigating this period will require careful public relations strategy and perhaps some visible gestures of appreciation toward fans who feel unsettled by the news.

The Broader Question Of Idol Dating

This incident has reignited ongoing debates about the expectations placed on K-pop idols regarding their personal lives. The traditional industry position that idols should remain publicly single to maintain fan fantasy relationships has been increasingly challenged in recent years, particularly by international fans with different cultural expectations.

Younger Korean fans have also begun pushing back against dating taboos, arguing that the restrictions are unreasonable and harmful to both idols and fan culture. These evolving attitudes suggest that while dating news still generates controversy, the intensity of backlash may be diminishing compared to previous generations.

Still, the commercial realities of K-pop—where fan spending is often motivated by parasocial attachment—mean that agencies remain cautious about officially confirming relationships. The tension between artist autonomy and business considerations continues to shape how dating news is managed.

What Happens Next

As of this writing, neither Yujin nor Moon Sangmin has made any personal statements about the dating rumors. Both continue their professional activities without visible changes, maintaining the typical celebrity strategy of not engaging with speculation.

Whether the rumors are true, the public response will likely influence how similar situations are handled in the future. If the couple (if they are a couple) weathers the storm without significant damage to either career, it may encourage other celebrities to be less secretive about their personal lives.

For now, fans on all sides continue to debate, analyze, and obsess over every scrap of potential evidence—a reminder of the intense public interest that accompanies celebrity lives in the digital age.
