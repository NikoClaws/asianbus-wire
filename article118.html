BIGBANG Launches 20th Anniversary With Coachella Showcase — Justin Bieber Makes Surprise Appearance

By Ji-won Lee April 13, 2026

INDIO, CALIFORNIA — BIGBANG returned to the Coachella Valley on Saturday night for the first time since 2017, launching their 20th debut anniversary with a headline set at the Outdoor Theatre that drew a massive crowd and earned some unexpected reinforcement on stage.

The K-pop pioneers — G-Dragon, Taeyang, Daesung, and Seungri — took the stage from 10:30 PM to 11:30 PM PST, delivering a high-energy set packed with their signature party anthems. According to the Los Angeles Times, BIGBANG "drew a huge crowd with a set long on sexed-up party jams and exposed abdominal muscles — one more sign of the festival's increasing internationalization in its own 25th year of business."

Justin Bieber's Surprise Appearance

The night's biggest surprise came mid-set when global pop star Justin Bieber joined BIGBANG on stage for an impromptu collaboration. Bieber performed his hit single "Baby" alongside the K-pop group, creating a historic crossover moment that had both audiences roaring. Fan-recorded clips of the collaboration immediately went viral across social media platforms.

The appearance was unexpected even by Coachella standards — no announcement had hinted at Bieber joining BIGBANG's set, making the cameo a genuine surprise for the packed Outdoor Theatre crowd.

20 Years In The Making

The Coachella showcase marks a significant milestone for BIGBANG, who debuted under YG Entertainment in 2006 and went on to become one of the most influential acts in modern pop music. Their return to the festival — this time without former member T.O.P, who recently released his solo album Another Dimension — was positioned as a celebration of their two-decade legacy.

BIGBANG previously performed at Coachella in 2017, becoming one of the first K-pop groups to play a major US festival at that scale. The 2026 return reinforces their continued global relevance and sets the stage for what YG Entertainment has described as a major anniversary year.

BIGBANG is scheduled to return for Coachella's second weekend on April 19, closing out the festival's Outdoor Theatre once again.
