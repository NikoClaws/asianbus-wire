BREAKING March 31, 2026

2026 MAMA Awards Confirmed For Osaka's Kyocera Dome — November 20-21

K-pop's biggest awards ceremony returns to Japan for a third consecutive year, with CJ ENM promising new on-site and online fan experiences streaming live worldwide via Mnet Plus.

MAMA Awards promotional image. Photo: CJ ENM

CJ ENM has officially confirmed that the 2026 MAMA Awards will be held at Kyocera Dome Osaka in Japan on November 20 and 21, with title sponsor Visa returning for the flagship K-pop ceremony. The two-day event will stream live worldwide via Mnet Plus, continuing the awards show's mission of connecting global fans beyond borders.

The announcement, made Tuesday by the leading entertainment company, marks the third consecutive time the ceremony will take place at the iconic Osaka venue. The MAMA Awards have become the most prestigious awards show in the K-pop industry and one of the largest music events in Asia.

27 Years Of "Music Makes ONE"

First launched in 1999 as Korea's first music video ceremony by CJ ENM's music channel Mnet, the show rebranded from "Mnet Asian Music Awards" to "MAMA AWARDS" in 2009, underscoring its commitment to becoming a truly global event. Since then, MAMA has hosted ceremonies across the world, including Macao, Singapore, Hong Kong, Vietnam, Japan, and the United States.

MAMA Awards Quick Facts:

• Dates: November 20-21, 2026
• Venue: Kyocera Dome Osaka, Japan
• Title Sponsor: Visa
• Live Stream: Mnet Plus (worldwide)
• Running Since: 1999 (27 years)
• Previous Locations: Korea, Japan, Hong Kong, Vietnam, Singapore, Macao, USA

A Historic Milestone In 2024

The awards show reached a major milestone in 2024 when it hosted its ceremony in the United States for the first time before continuing in Japan. That edition went on to win the Best General Entertainment Programme award at the Asian Academy Creative Awards in 2025, cementing MAMA's status as not just a K-pop event but a globally recognized entertainment spectacle.

"The MAMA Awards has elevated K-pop's value and vision for 27 years, honoring not just excellence in music, but also those who broaden K-pop's reach and redefine its possibilities on the world stage."

– CJ ENM

New Experiences Planned For 2026

This year's edition promises to expand beyond the stage with new on-site and online events designed to deliver what CJ ENM calls "a truly borderless experience." While specific details about performers and nominees haven't been announced yet, the organizer says more information will be unveiled in the coming months.

With 2026 shaping up to be one of the biggest years in K-pop history — BTS's massive comeback, a wave of April comebacks from LE SSERAFIM, HWASA, and 82MAJOR, plus continued momentum from fourth-generation groups — the competition for this year's MAMA trophies is already looking fierce.

What Fans Need To Know

Ticket sales and the full lineup of presenters and performers have yet to be announced. Fans are advised to follow MAMA Awards' official channels and Mnet Plus for updates. Given the venue's 36,000+ capacity and the global demand for K-pop events in 2026, tickets are expected to sell out rapidly once they go on sale.

For fans who can't make it to Osaka, the live worldwide stream via Mnet Plus ensures no one misses the action. The platform has expanded significantly in recent years, making MAMA one of the most accessible major awards shows in the music industry.
