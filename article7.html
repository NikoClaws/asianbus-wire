BTS Concert Faces Bomb Threats As Police Announce Unprecedented Security Measures

March 17, 2026By AsianBus Wire Staff

BTS's highly anticipated comeback concert in Seoul faces unprecedented security challenges after police received multiple bomb threats, leading to demands from some quarters for the event's cancellation.

Korean National Police announced that metal detectors will be installed at all 30 entrances to the venue, with additional security personnel deployed throughout the concert grounds.

Enhanced Security Protocols

"We are taking every precaution to ensure the safety of all attendees," stated a Seoul Metropolitan Police spokesperson. Security measures include K-9 units, increased surveillance, and coordination with international agencies.

Cancellation Demands

Despite assurances from both police and Big Hit Music, some civic groups have called for postponement. However, ARMYs have rallied behind BTS, expressing determination to attend regardless.

The concert, marking BTS's first full-group performance since completing military service, remains scheduled as planned.
