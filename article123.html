BTS's "SWIM" Becomes 1st K-Pop MV of 2026 to Hit 100 Million Views

April 15, 2026 By Julian Choi 📖 5 min read

BTS has achieved another remarkable YouTube milestone. On April 15 at approximately 1 p.m. KST, the group's music video for "SWIM" officially surpassed 100 million views, making it the first K-pop music video released in 2026 to reach this prestigious milestone.

The record-breaking feat comes just 26 days after the music video's original release on March 20 at 1 p.m. KST, demonstrating BTS's enduring global popularity and the song's widespread appeal among fans and casual viewers alike.

The achievement places "SWIM" among BTS's fastest-paced music videos this year, with the group continuing to dominate both Korean and international music charts. The MV's vibrant summer aesthetic and catchy choreography have resonated strongly with audiences worldwide.

"SWIM" represents one of BTS's most ambitious visual releases, featuring sweeping cinematic sequences and elaborate set designs that have sparked viral discussion across social media platforms since release. Fan edits and dance covers have flooded TikTok and YouTube, further amplifying the track's reach.

A Historic 2026 Run

The 100-million-view milestone is particularly significant given the increasingly competitive landscape of K-pop releases in 2026. Despite new comebacks from multiple top acts, BTS remains the only group to achieve a centum-view MV this year, solidifying their status as streaming giants in the digital age.

Fans celebrated the achievement across social media, with hashtags related to the milestone trending worldwide within hours of the announcement. ARMY members organized coordinated streaming parties to push the video over the final threshold.

What's Next for BTS

Following the success of "SWIM" and their record-breaking Billboard 200 performances with "Arirang," BTS shows no signs of slowing down. The group is currently on their ongoing world tour, with additional dates expected to be announced for later in the year.

Congratulations to BTS on this historic achievement!
