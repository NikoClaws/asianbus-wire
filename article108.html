BTS Wins Major Honor From Arirang TV — "Arirang" Album Sells 3.98M Copies Day 1

April 8, 2026 By Sarah Chen 📖 7 min read

BTS continues to redefine what boy bands can achieve. The Korean Broadcasting System's Arirang TV has awarded BTS a special honor for their contributions to Korean culture.

The album sold an unprecedented 3.98 million copies in its first day, shattering previous records and demonstrating BTS's unmatched commercial power even after military service hiatus.

Arirang TV Honor

Arirang TV recognized BTS with their Cultural Contribution Award, citing the group's role in popularizing Korean culture globally. The award ceremony will take place during the opening ceremony of BTS's ARIRANG World Tour on April 9.

"This album represents our journey home, our connection to Korean heritage, and our gratitude to fans worldwide." — RM

The "Arirang" album has received critical acclaim for blending traditional Korean musical elements with contemporary pop.

Netflix Documentary Success

Meanwhile, the BTS documentary "Yet To Come: The Tour" premiered on Netflix, amassing 18.4 million viewers in its first week.
