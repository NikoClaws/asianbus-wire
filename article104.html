BLACKPINK Announces 2026 World Tour Dates — Ticket Sale Details Inside

The iconic K-pop quartet is set to embark on their biggest world tour yet, with stadium shows across four continents

By Sarah Chen • April 4, 2026
8 min read

BLACKPINK is officially making their return to the global stage in 2026 with an ambitious world tour that promises to be their most extensive yet. YG Entertainment confirmed the tour dates during a special announcement broadcast from their Seoul headquarters.

The tour, titled "BORN PINK AGAIN," will kick off in Seoul's Olympic Stadium on May 15, 2026, before expanding to stadium shows across North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and Oceania. The announcement comes on the heels of their record-breaking "Born Pink" tour, which became the highest-grossing tour by a K-pop girl group in history.

North American Leg

BLINKs in the United States and Canada will have multiple opportunities to catch the group live, with shows scheduled in major cities including Los Angeles, New York, Chicago, Toronto, and Mexico City. The North American leg alone spans 12 stadium dates, reflecting the group's meteoric rise in Western markets since their 2019 Coachella appearance.

"This tour is for every BLINK around the world. We've prepared something truly special that celebrates our journey together." — Jisoo

Tickets for the North American dates will go on sale starting April 15 through Ticketmaster, with Verified Fan registration opening on April 10. The group has implemented a fan-first ticketing system to combat scalping, similar to their previous tour.

European Expansion

For the first time in their career, BLACKPINK will play stadium shows across the United Kingdom, France, Germany, Spain, and the Netherlands. London fans can expect a two-night residency at Wembley Stadium, while Paris gets a rare stadium date at the Stade de France.

Tour Dates

May 15-17: Seoul, South Korea

May 24-25: Tokyo, Japan

June 5-6: Los Angeles, USA

June 12-13: New York, USA

June 20-21: London, UK

June 28: Paris, France

About BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. They are one of the most successful K-pop groups internationally.
