BTS' Kim Taehyung Returns to Acting with Short Film 'Inner' Releasing Today

V makes his acting comeback with a heartfelt short film following his ARIRANG album success

By Sarah Chen • April 10, 2026
5 min read

BTS' Kim Taehyung, known worldwide as V, is making his much-anticipated return to acting. The short film "Inner" is set to release today, April 10, 2026, marking V's first on-screen project since his acclaimed performance in the 2022 drama "One Fine Day."

The announcement comes on the heels of V's successful solo album "ARIRANG" which has been receiving critical acclaim and commercial success since its release. Fans have been eagerly awaiting this announcement, having seen hints of V's acting pursuits during various broadcasts.

A Poster Worth a Million Words

A poster released earlier this week featured V in a school uniform, sitting on a front-row bench — an image that instantly went viral across social media platforms. The poster generated millions of interactions within hours, with fans praising V's timeless visuals and emotional expression.

The short film is described as a heartfelt story about self-discovery and the internal struggles many young adults face. Industry insiders who have seen the project have called it "emotionally powerful" and "a breakthrough in V's acting career."

V's Acting Journey

V debuted as an actor with "One Fine Day," a romantic drama that showcased his natural acting ability. The series earned him critical praise and a dedicated fanbase as an actor, not just a singer. His return to the screen has been highly anticipated by both fans and critics.

"Inner" is set to premiere at midnight KST tonight, with a global release following shortly after. A special screening for fans is being organized in Seoul, though details remain under wraps.

About Kim Taehyung (V)

Kim Taehyung, known as V, is a member of BTS and a solo artist. He debuted as an actor with "One Fine Day" and is known for his deep baritone voice and versatile acting skills.
