Ha Hyun Sang Drops Second Studio Album 'New Boat'

April 6, 2026 · AsianBus Wire

K-ballad star Ha Hyun Sang is back with his most ambitious project yet. His second full-length studio album New Boat drops April 6 at 6PM KST across all major streaming platforms.

The announcement, made on March 19, 2026, set off a wave of anticipation among fans who have been eagerly awaiting the follow-up to his successful debut. To build momentum, Ha Hyun Sang has been holding a special archive live concert series — '2026 HA HYUN SANG Archive Live' — at Olympic Hall in Seoul Olympic Park from April 3-5, giving fans an exclusive preview of the new material in an intimate setting.

Second full-length album New Boat releasing April 6 at 6PM KST. Preceded by a 3-day archive live concert series at Olympic Hall, Seoul.

The album scheduler and tracklist were unveiled in the weeks leading up to release, teasing what fans describe as "the most emotionally raw and sonically ambitious work of his career." Ha Hyun Sang, known for his powerful ballad vocals and deeply personal songwriting, appears to be taking his artistry to new heights with this release.

Following the album drop and archive live concerts, Ha Hyun Sang is also set to appear on major festival stages, including Lovesome Festival 2026 — continuing what promises to be a significant year for the acclaimed artist.

New Boat marks a major milestone in Ha Hyun Sang's career as he continues to establish himself as one of K-pop's most compelling vocalists and songwriters.
