CORTIS 'GREENGREEN' Shatters Records With 1.22 Million Pre-Orders

April 5, 2026 · AsianBus Wire

The K-pop industry is witnessing a historic moment as CORTIS announces their upcoming second mini-album GREENGREEN has officially crossed the 1.22 million mark in pre-orders — achieved in just one week.

The staggering figure, released by distributors YG Plus and Universal Records on April 4, marks a monumental milestone for the five-member group, solidifying their transition from rising stars to global music leaders.

1,227,986 copies pre-ordered as of April 2 — just 7 days after pre-orders opened on March 27. That's nearly triple the first-week sales of their debut album COLOR OUTSIDE THE LINES (436,367 copies).

By the end of March, the "Pre-save" count for GREENGREEN on Spotify had already surpassed 565,000, signaling a massive global audience waiting for the new tracks.

Big Hit Music has announced a strategic teaser schedule, with the pre-release of the title track "REDRED" dropping April 20 at 6PM KST, accompanied by a music video and conceptual performance film.

The label described the upcoming album as a "deeply personal project" reflecting the individual tastes and artistic directions of all five members — a shift toward self-expression that fans have eagerly anticipated following their eight-month hiatus.

But the momentum doesn't stop there. CORTIS has also been officially confirmed to perform at Lollapalooza Chicago in August 2026 — the only K-pop boy group included in this year's star-studded lineup, and a major launching pad for their North American expansion.

With a full month remaining until the official release on May 4, 2026, industry watchers expect the total pre-order volume to continue climbing — potentially setting even higher records before the physical albums even hit shelves.
