BLACKPINK Announces Official Comeback Date After Rosé's Solo Success

April 9, 2026 By Ji-won Lee 📖 6 min read

BLACKPINK is officially making their highly anticipated return. YG Entertainment confirmed the group's comeback date during a special press event on April 9, 2026.

The announcement comes on the heels of Rosé's successful solo debut, which broke records across multiple streaming platforms and topped charts in over 30 countries. Her accomplishment seems to have energized the group's own comeback preparations.

The Comeback Timeline

YG Entertainment revealed that BLACKPINK will release their new album on July 15, 2026, with a full world tour to follow. The album, titled "RUBY x DIAMOND," marks the group's first major release since their record-breaking "BORN PINK" era in 2022.

Industry analysts suggest the timing is strategic, positioning BLACKPINK to dominate the summer concert season.

What We Know About the Album

While YG has kept specific details under wraps, sources close to the group indicate the album will feature a mix of hip-hop influenced tracks and ballad-style songs.

Rosé's Solo Success Paved the Way

Rosé's debut solo album "ROSIE" has spent three consecutive weeks in the top 5 of the Billboard 200. The album's lead single "broken diamonds" became her most successful solo effort to date.
