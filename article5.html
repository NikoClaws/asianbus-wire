ASTRO's Cha Eunwoo To Push Forward With First Public Event Since Tax Evasion Allegations

March 17, 2026 By AsianBus Wire Staff

ASTRO member and acclaimed actor Cha Eunwoo is set to make his first public appearance since being embroiled in tax evasion allegations, with a photo exhibition event scheduled in Japan.

The "Face Genius" idol-actor has kept a low profile following revelations about entertainment industry tax audits that implicated several high-profile celebrities. Now, his agency appears ready to test public sentiment with a carefully planned return to the spotlight.

The Tax Evasion Scandal

Earlier this year, Korean tax authorities revealed staggering figures relating to entertainment agency audits, with several major companies and individual celebrities facing scrutiny. While Cha Eunwoo was not the only celebrity implicated, his squeaky-clean image made the allegations particularly damaging to his reputation.

The exact details of Cha Eunwoo's case have not been made fully public, leading to rampant speculation across Korean online communities. Some fans have defended him vigorously, while others have expressed disappointment.

Japan Photo Exhibition

The upcoming photo exhibition in Japan represents a strategic choice for his return. Japanese fans have historically been supportive of Korean celebrities even during controversies, and the event allows for a controlled environment where Cha Eunwoo can reconnect with supporters.

"Japan has always been a second home for K-Pop artists," noted one industry analyst. "It's a smart move to gauge fan reaction in a more forgiving market before returning to Korean schedules."

Fan Reactions

AROHA (ASTRO's fandom) reactions have been mixed. Many fans are excited to see Cha Eunwoo return to activities, expressing their unwavering support. Others have called for a more thorough explanation of the allegations before they can fully move on.

"We need transparency," wrote one fan. "If he addresses this directly and takes responsibility, I think most fans will forgive and move forward. But the silence has been hard."

What's Next

The success of the Japan event could determine the trajectory of Cha Eunwoo's career recovery. Industry watchers will be paying close attention to attendance numbers and media coverage as indicators of whether the public is ready to welcome him back.

Cha Eunwoo's agency has not commented on future Korean activities or any potential statements addressing the tax allegations directly.
