ASTRO's Cha Eunwoo Returns to Public Eye Amid Tax Evasion Scandal

March 17, 2026By AsianBus Wire Staff

The "Face Genius" idol-actor Cha Eunwoo is set to make his first public appearance since being embroiled in tax evasion allegations that have threatened to derail his stellar career.

Cha Eunwoo, real name Lee Dong-min, has maintained a low profile since news broke of an investigation into his financial affairs. Now, the ASTRO member appears ready to address the controversy head-on.

The Allegations

Reports surfaced last month alleging that Cha Eunwoo had underreported income from his numerous brand endorsements and acting projects. The National Tax Service reportedly launched an investigation into the matter.

Public Reaction

Fans have been divided, with many expressing unwavering support while others demand accountability. His agency, Fantagio, has stated they are cooperating fully with authorities.

Industry experts note that how Cha Eunwoo handles this situation could set a precedent for how K-celebrities navigate financial controversies in the future.
