CORTIS 'GREENGREEN' Shatters Records With 1.22 Million Pre-Orders

April 5, 2026 By Min-ji Park 📖 6 min read

CORTIS has officially shattered all expectations. The rookie group's debut album "GREENGREEN" has accumulated over 1.22 million pre-orders, setting a new record for debut releases.

The achievement places CORTIS among the most successful debut acts in K-pop history, with their numbers rivaling established acts rather than first-time artists. The label was caught off guard by the overwhelming response.

A Social Media Phenomenon

The group's rise to fame has been fueled by social media, with clips of their pre-debut content amassing millions of views. Their unique concept and distinct sound resonated with audiences before they even officially debuted.

Fans have organized massive streaming parties and pre-order campaigns that broke multiple platform records. The CORTIS fandom has proven to be one of the most dedicated in recent memory.

Overwhelming Industry Response

Industry insiders have expressed disbelief at the numbers. "We've seen strong debuts before, but nothing like this. CORTIS has tapped into something that transcends typical K-pop fan culture," one analyst commented.

The album is set to release on April 15, with the group preparing an elaborate showcase event that will be broadcast globally.
