BTS Jungkook's "Personality Change" Sparks Heated Debate Among ARMYs

March 17, 2026By AsianBus Wire Staff

BTS's golden maknae Jungkook finds himself at the center of controversy following a viral post and recent drunk live broadcast that has fans questioning whether their beloved idol has changed.

The debate began when a Korean online community post went viral, compiling instances of Jungkook's recent behavior that posters claimed showed a dramatic shift from his previous personality. The post garnered thousands of comments, with opinions sharply divided.

The Drunk Live Incident

Adding fuel to the fire was Jungkook's recent live broadcast, during which he appeared visibly intoxicated. While some fans found the broadcast endearing and authentic, others expressed concern about the idol's well-being and questioned whether such behavior was appropriate for someone of his influence.

During the broadcast, Jungkook made several comments that were later analyzed and debated extensively on social media. Translations varied, leading to confusion about his actual statements and intentions.

ARMY Divided

The BTS fandom, known as ARMY, has found itself split on the issue. Longtime fans have rushed to Jungkook's defense, arguing that he's simply maturing and becoming more comfortable showing his true self.

"He's a grown man who's been in the spotlight since he was 15," wrote one defender. "Let him live his life."

Critics, however, argue that the changes go beyond normal maturation. Some have pointed to his recent solo activities and time away from the group as potential factors.
