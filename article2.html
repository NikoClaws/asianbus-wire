BTS Jungkook's "Personality Change" Sparks Heated Debate Among ARMYs

March 17, 2026 By AsianBus Wire Staff

BTS's golden maknae Jungkook has found himself at the center of controversy this week following a viral post questioning changes in his public persona, along with a recent "drunk live" broadcast that has divided the fandom.

The conversation began when a viral post on Korean online communities began circulating, with netizens expressing concerns about Jungkook's apparent change in demeanor. The post, which has since been viewed millions of times, compared his earlier public appearances to more recent ones, suggesting a shift in his personality.

The Drunk Live Incident

Adding fuel to the fire, Jungkook's recent live broadcast while appearing intoxicated has sparked intense discussion. Fans have noted that his appearance in new "ARIRANG" video content seems to be linked to the controversial live stream, with some speculating about what schedule could have prompted a 9 AM appearance in such a state.

"We just want to know he's okay," wrote one concerned fan. "The Jungkook we're seeing lately seems different from the one we've known for years."

Defenders Speak Out

However, many ARMYs have rushed to Jungkook's defense, arguing that he's simply maturing as an artist and that scrutinizing his personal behavior is invasive and unfair.

"He's 28 years old now," pointed out one defender. "He's been in the spotlight since he was 15. If he wants to have a drink and chat with fans, that's his business. People change and grow — that's normal and healthy."

BTS's Upcoming Concert

Meanwhile, attention is also turning to safety measures being implemented for BTS's highly anticipated comeback concert. The extensive precautions have become a hot topic among fans eager to see the group perform together again.

Big Hit Music has not issued any official statement regarding the controversies surrounding Jungkook, maintaining their typical stance of allowing members privacy in personal matters.

As BTS prepares for their return to the stage, all eyes will be on Jungkook to see how he navigates this latest chapter in his decade-long career.
