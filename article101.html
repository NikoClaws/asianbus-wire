BTS Announced as Headliners for Global Citizen Festival 2026

The K-pop superstars make their triumphant return to the international festival stage

By Jennifer Lopez • April 1, 2026
6 min read

The wait is finally over. BTS has been officially announced as one of the headlining acts for the Global Citizen Festival 2026, set to take place in Central Park, New York City. The announcement, made during a live broadcast by Global Citizen, marks BTS's first major international festival appearance since their hiatus began in 2023.

The festival, known for its advocacy-driven approach and high-profile musical performances, will see BTS join an impressive lineup of international artists. The group's performance is expected to draw attention to their ongoing campaign for generational mental health awareness, a cause they've championed throughout their career.

A Meaningful Return

BTS's association with Global Citizen isn't new. In 2021, they delivered a powerful speech at the UN General Assembly, urging young people to get vaccinated. Their continued partnership with the organization underscores their commitment to making a positive impact beyond entertainment.

"Music has the power to bring people together, and we're honored to be part of an event that uses that power for good." — RM

The festival will be broadcast globally, allowing BLINKs and Global Citizens from around the world to be part of the event. Tickets for the festival will be available through Global Citizen's ticket lottery system, with proceeds supporting various educational and environmental initiatives.

Event Details

Date: September 28, 2026

Venue: Central Park, NYC

Time: 2:00 PM EST

About BTS

BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan) is a South Korean boy band consisting of RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. They are known for their social activism and record-breaking music.
