Ex-ENHYPEN Member Heeseung Debuts Solo Under New Name 'Evan'

April 11, 2026 By Min-ji Park 📖 5 min read

Former ENHYPEN member Heeseung is officially debuting as a solo artist under the new name 'Evan,' marking a new chapter in his career just weeks after his departure from the K-pop group he helped launch in 2020.

The news comes as Heeseung wrapped up his activities with ENHYPEN in early April 2026. His exit, announced by Belift Lab in March, was described as the result of "deep discussions" about the group's future direction and each member's individual career aspirations. Now, the former ENHYPEN member is charting his own path forward.

Who Is Evan?

Heeseung, whose full name is Lee Heeseung, debuted as a member of ENHYPEN in 2020 after appearing on the survival show I-LAND. As one of the group's most recognized vocalists, he quickly became known for his powerful performances and emotional delivery. His departure from ENHYPEN marked the end of a six-year run with the group that saw them become one of K-pop's most successful fourth-generation acts.

The decision to adopt a new stage name signals a deliberate break from his ENHYPEN identity. Rather than continuing under "Heeseung," the artist will be known as Evan in his solo work — a name that insiders say reflects his desire to present a different side of himself as a solo artist.

What To Expect From His Solo Work

Just weeks after his departure announcement, Evan has already hinted at new music on the horizon. In posts on his personal social media accounts — which have been rebranded to reflect his new identity — he teased an upcoming solo album that he described as "the most honest work I've ever done."

Industry observers are watching closely to see how his sound evolves outside the group context. Heeseung was known for his versatility as a vocalist, capable of both powerful high notes and soft, emotional ballads. His solo project is expected to lean into a more personal, introspective style that reflects his individual artistry rather than ENHYPEN's typically high-energy pop sound.

Belift Lab, his new agency, has described the upcoming project as "a new beginning" for the artist, emphasizing that his solo work will give him greater creative control over both his music and artistic direction. The agency added that fans can expect "a different kind of performance" compared to what they saw from him in ENHYPEN.

Fan Reactions

Fans of Heeseung — who refer to themselves as ENSEMBLE — have expressed a mixture of excitement and bittersweet emotions. Many are thrilled to see him pursue solo work but acknowledge the sadness of watching him leave the group that launched his career. Social media has been flooded with tributes to his ENHYPEN era alongside anticipation for what Evan will bring as a solo act.

The debut of Evan marks one of the most notable solo departures from a K-pop group in recent memory, and the success — or failure — of his solo project will be closely watched as an indicator of how labels manage artist transitions from group to solo work in an increasingly competitive market.
