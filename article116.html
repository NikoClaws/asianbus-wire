Ex-ENHYPEN Heeseung Debuts Solo As 'Evan' — New Profile Photos Unveiled

By Ji-won Lee April 11, 2026

SEOUL — The wait is over. Former ENHYPEN member Heeseung, who announced his departure from the group in March 2026, has officially launched his solo career under a new stage name: Evan.

On April 9, 2026, Belift Lab unveiled a set of profile images for the artist now known as Evan, describing the concept as one that "focuses on simplicity, featuring minimal styling and a natural look" — a deliberate departure from the high-energy, concept-driven aesthetic of ENHYPEN.

The singer also debuted a new social media account under the name Evan, sharing several photos with fans. Belift Lab said the new images represent the artist in his "most authentic form," signaling a reset of both identity and artistic direction.

A New Name, A New Sound

The decision to drop his birth name stage name and adopt an entirely new identity is rare in K-pop, where most solo artists maintain at least a variation of their group-era name. Sources close to the project say the rebranding reflects both Heeseung's desire to distance himself from his ENHYPEN chapter and his ambition to develop music that differs significantly from what fans heard during his six years with the group.

ENHYPEN's official statement upon his March 10 departure noted that Heeseung would be "transitioning to a solo career through individual activities," and that Belift Lab would support his solo pursuits. The agency's swift rollout of his new brand suggests the project has been in development for some time.

No details have been announced yet regarding his second debut or a debut single. But given the scale of the rebrand — new name, new concept, new social presence — industry observers expect his solo music to arrive within the coming months.

Fans React to the Rebrand

Fans of both ENHYPEN and K-pop generally have responded with a mix of excitement and nostalgia. Many ENHYPEN fans expressed happiness that Heeseung's next chapter is taking shape, while others noted the bittersweetness of seeing him fully shed the identity fans knew for six years.

"Heeseung was one of the most talented performers in ENHYPEN — his vocal color is instantly recognizable," one fan wrote on X. "Evan sounds like a fresh start. I can't wait to hear what he does with it."

Heeseung debuted with ENHYPEN in 2020 and quickly established himself as one of the group's core vocalists and performers. His departure in March made him the first member to leave the group since its debut, and remains one of the most talked-about K-pop exits of 2026.
