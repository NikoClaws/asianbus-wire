Girls' Generation Tiffany Shows Off Wedding Ring, Opens Up About Byun Yo Han Romance

March 17, 2026By AsianBus Wire Staff

Tiffany Young has finally given fans a glimpse of her stunning wedding ring following her marriage to acclaimed actor Byun Yo Han, marking one of K-entertainment's most celebrated unions.

The Girls' Generation member, 36, shared photos on her Instagram showing off an elegant diamond ring that has fans and jewelry experts alike buzzing.

A Private Ceremony

True to their private nature, Tiffany and Byun Yo Han tied the knot in an intimate ceremony attended only by close family and friends. The wedding reportedly took place at a private venue in Seoul.

Fellow Girls' Generation members were in attendance, with the group posting a touching congratulatory message that moved fans to tears.

Their Love Story

The couple was first rumored to be dating in late 2024, though both remained tight-lipped about their relationship. Their connection reportedly began through mutual friends in the entertainment industry.

Byun Yo Han, known for his roles in acclaimed dramas, has been praised for his supportive stance toward Tiffany's career. The actor, 39, previously expressed admiration for her work ethic.
