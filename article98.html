TXT Drops Whimsical New Teasers For '7TH YEAR: A Moment of Stillness in the Thorns' — April 13 Comeback Confirmed

April 2, 2026 3 min read By Jisoo Lee

SEOUL — TOMORROW X TOGETHER is building serious anticipation for their April comeback with a stunning new set of concept teasers. The five-member group dropped the latest visuals for their eighth mini album, 7TH YEAR: A Moment of Stillness in the Thorns, showing the members stranded in a dreamy, whimsical world that looks like something straight out of a storybook.

The album is officially set to drop on April 13 at 6 PM KST, marking a special milestone as TXT celebrates seven years since their 2019 debut under BIGHIT MUSIC.

A fairytale gone wild

The new concept photos lean heavily into a fantastical aesthetic — overgrown forests, muted pastels, and a sense of being lost in a world between dream and reality. Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun, and Huening Kai appear in outfits that blend vintage elegance with a slightly disheveled, adventurer-lost-in-the-woods energy.

It's a visual departure from the darker, more cinematic tones of their previous releases, and fans are loving the shift. "The storytelling in these teasers is insane," one fan wrote on social media. "TXT never misses with concept photos."

April's stacked comeback calendar

TXT's return comes amid one of the busiest April comeback schedules in recent K-pop memory. KISS OF LIFE drops their new project on April 6, MONSTA X releases full album Unfold on April 3, and KickFlip makes their debut alongside &TEAM and BABYMONSTER releases later in the month.

But TXT's anniversary comeback has carved out a special lane. The group has been teasing the album since late March, building a carefully paced rollout of concept photos, mood trailers, and cryptic social media posts that have kept MOA (their fandom) on high alert.

What to expect

While BIGHIT hasn't revealed the full tracklist yet, the album title and concept suggest a reflective, introspective project. The "thorns" imagery paired with the whimsical setting hints at a story about growth through pain — a fitting theme for a seven-year anniversary release.

Pre-orders for 7TH YEAR: A Moment of Stillness in the Thorns are already live, and with April 13 fast approaching, expect more teasers — including a likely MV teaser — in the coming days.
