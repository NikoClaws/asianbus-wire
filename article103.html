KATSEYE's Manon Breaks Silence Amid 'Pinky Up' Comeback — Removes Group Name From Instagram Bio

KATSEYE's Manon Breaks Silence Amid 'Pinky Up' Comeback — Removes Group Name From Instagram Bio

By Staff Writer • April 2026
6 min read

The update came on Thursday as KATSEYE dropped the first teaser for their upcoming single "Pinky Up" — notably without Manon. The track, set to release April 9, marks the group's first new music since Manon announced her temporary hiatus in February to focus on her health and wellbeing.

'I'm Happy And I'm Healthy'

In her Weverse statement, Manon thanked fans — known as EYEKONS — for their patience. "Thank you so much for all the love and support you've been sending my way," she wrote. "I'm really grateful for the patience and kindness everyone has shown during this time."

She went on to address the elephant in the room: her relationship with HYBE x Geffen, the label behind KATSEYE. "HxG and I are having positive conversations and I feel supported," she continued, adding: "I'm happy and I'm healthy. I'll share more soon."

Instagram Bio Change Fuels Exit Rumors

The statement came after eagle-eyed fans noticed that Manon had quietly updated her Instagram bio, replacing any mention of KATSEYE with simply "mademoiselle manzanita." The change immediately sent social media into a frenzy, with many fans fearing a permanent departure.

However, Manon's reassuring Weverse message appears to have calmed some concerns — at least for now. Her note that she's in "positive conversations" with management suggests negotiations may still be ongoing rather than finalized.

KATSEYE Pushes Forward With 'Pinky Up'

Meanwhile, the remaining five members of KATSEYE have been busy rolling out a full promotional campaign for "Pinky Up." Teasers so far have included a cryptic clip featuring the Mona Lisa with colorful nails sipping tea, followed by the official track title and release date announcement.

The group has also shared a complete rollout schedule building up to their highly anticipated Coachella debut. Since Manon's hiatus began, KATSEYE has continued performing as a quintet at international festivals including Lollapalooza Brazil and Chile.

Whether Manon will eventually rejoin KATSEYE remains the biggest question in K-pop right now. For fans, her message offers hope — but the Instagram bio change keeps the uncertainty very much alive.
