BTS Earns Three Nominations at 2026 American Music Awards — Including Artist of the Year

April 15, 2026 Julian Choi 4 min read

BTS has secured three major nominations at the 2026 American Music Awards, including the prestigious Artist of the Year category — one of the most competitive fields in the ceremony's history.

The South Korean septet earned nominations for Artist of the Year, Song of the Summer, and Best Male K-Pop Artist at the 52nd American Music Awards, which will broadcast live from Las Vegas on May 25 at 8 PM ET / 5 PM PT.

Known for chart-topping hits and record-breaking streams, BTS faces stiff competition in the Artist of the Year category, where Taylor Swift leads with eight nominations, followed by Morgan Wallen, Sabrina Carpenter, and Olivia Dean — each with seven nods. BTS is grouped alongside pop heavyweights including Lady Gaga (6 nominations), Justin Bieber, and Bruno Mars.

"Song of the Summer" places BTS alongside artists like Harry Styles, Bad Bunny, and PinkPantheress, while the Best Male K-Pop Artist category puts them ahead of other notable acts in the genre.

A Consistent Awards Show Force

This year's AMAs nominations further cement BTS's status as one of the defining pop acts of their generation. The group has previously dominated both the AMAs and Billboard Music Awards, winning multiple Artist of the Year-adjacent categories in prior years.

The nomination comes as BTS continues to release solo and group work, with several members maintaining solo careers while the full group remains active in the broader K-pop landscape.

Fans worldwide — known as ARMY — are rallying on social media ahead of the May 25 ceremony, trending #BTSARMYForAOTY across platforms and organizing voter mobilization efforts ahead of the fan-voted categories.

The 2026 AMAs will broadcast live from Las Vegas's MGM Grand Garden Arena on May 25. Voting opens in May.
