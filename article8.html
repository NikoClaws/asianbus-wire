LE SSERAFIM Members Made "Uncomfortable" During Recent Fan Event — Sparking Fury

March 17, 2026By AsianBus Wire Staff

LE SSERAFIM members were visibly distressed during a recent fan signing event after attendees engaged in behavior that fans are calling "completely unacceptable."

Videos from the event show members appearing uncomfortable and exchanging concerned glances. The footage has sparked intense debate about fan behavior and organizer responsibilities.

What Happened

According to eyewitness accounts, several attendees made inappropriate requests and comments. Those present described the atmosphere as "tense" and "uncomfortable."

HYBE's Response

Source Music released a brief statement: "We are reviewing our event protocols and will take appropriate measures." Critics have called this response insufficient.

Calls for blacklisting problematic attendees and implementing stricter vetting processes have gained significant traction online.
