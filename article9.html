BTS Jungkook And aespa Winter's "Favorite Date Spot" Exposed As Fans Debate Evidence

March 17, 2026By AsianBus Wire Staff

The K-Pop world is buzzing with speculation after alleged "evidence" of BTS's Jungkook and aespa's Winter dating surfaced, including claims they share a "favorite date spot."

Rumors began after eagle-eyed fans noticed alleged matching visits to the same Seoul restaurant, with some claiming the idols left signatures on the establishment's celebrity wall on the same date.

The "Evidence"

Posts claim to show photographs of signatures allegedly belonging to both idols. Additionally, fans have compiled a timeline of supposed sightings and "coincidental" social media activity.

Fan Reactions

Both ARMYs and MYs have been divided. "Even if it's true, they're adults who deserve privacy," argued one supporter. Critics have pointed out inconsistencies in the alleged evidence.

Neither Big Hit Music nor SM Entertainment have issued statements. Both agencies typically decline to comment on artists' personal lives.
