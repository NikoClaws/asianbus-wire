BTS Jungkook And aespa Winter's "Favorite Date Spot" Exposed As Fans Debate Evidence

March 17, 2026 By AsianBus Wire Staff

The K-Pop world is buzzing with speculation after alleged "evidence" of BTS's Jungkook and aespa's Winter dating surfaced online, including claims they share a "favorite date spot" and even left signatures together.

The rumors began circulating after eagle-eyed fans noticed what they claim are matching visits to the same Seoul restaurant, with some alleging the idols left signatures on the establishment's celebrity wall on the same date.

The "Evidence"

Posts spreading across social media claim to show photographs of signatures allegedly belonging to both idols at a high-end restaurant known for its private dining rooms. Additionally, fans have compiled a timeline of supposed sightings and "coincidental" social media activity.

"They even left signatures on the same page," claimed one viral post, though the authenticity of the images has been questioned by skeptics.

Heated Debate

Both ARMYs and MYs (aespa's fanbase) have been divided in their reactions. While some fans support the potential relationship, others have dismissed the evidence as fabricated or coincidental.

"Even if it's true, they're adults who deserve privacy," argued one supporter. "Let them live their lives."

Critics of the rumors have pointed out inconsistencies in the alleged evidence and accused certain accounts of deliberately stirring up controversy for engagement.

Agency Silence

Neither Big Hit Music nor SM Entertainment have issued statements regarding the rumors. Both agencies typically decline to comment on artists' personal lives unless directly confirmed by the idols themselves.

The speculation comes at a particularly busy time for both artists, with Jungkook preparing for BTS's group activities and Winter in the middle of aespa's ongoing promotions.

As with many K-Pop dating rumors, definitive proof remains elusive, leaving fans to debate the circumstantial evidence while both idols maintain their silence.
