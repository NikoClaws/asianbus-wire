NewJeans Reveals 'Tomorrow' Release Date — Hanni and Minji Tease 'Most Personal Album Yet'

April 6, 2026 By Ji-won Lee 📖 5 min read

NewJeans has finally revealed the details of their much-anticipated comeback. The group announced during a surprise livestream that their new album "Tomorrow" will drop on May 15, 2026.

The announcement sent shockwaves through the K-pop community, with fans immediately flooding social media with excitement. Hanni and Minji described the album as their "most personal work yet."

A New Direction

Hanni spoke about the creative process behind "Tomorrow," explaining that this album represents a significant artistic evolution. "We've always wanted to make music that feels true to us, but this time we went even deeper. Every song has a piece of our hearts in it," she said.

Minji elaborated on the themes explored in the album, hinting at more personal lyrics than previous releases. "There are songs about growing up, facing challenges, and staying true to yourself. We hope our fans can see themselves in these stories," she added.

Visual Concept

The group also teased a completely new visual direction that breaks from their signature Y2K aesthetic. While keeping some nostalgic elements, "Tomorrow" will introduce fresh styling and concepts that reflect the members' growth as artists.
