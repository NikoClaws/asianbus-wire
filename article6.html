"K-Pop Demon Hunters" Wins Oscar Amid Boos and BTS Controversy

March 17, 2026By AsianBus Wire Staff

The 2026 Academy Awards delivered one of its most controversial moments when "K-Pop Demon Hunters" took home an award amid audible boos and backlash related to BTS.

The Korean film, which features fictionalized versions of K-Pop idols battling supernatural forces, won Best Original Song. However, the acceptance speech was overshadowed by audience reaction.

The BTS Connection

Controversy stems from the film's perceived negative portrayal of characters resembling BTS members. ARMYs had campaigned against the film for months, arguing it disrespected the group.

Industry Impact

The incident has sparked conversations about K-Pop's representation in Western media and the power of dedicated fandoms in shaping entertainment industry narratives.
