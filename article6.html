"K-Pop Demon Hunters" Wins Oscar Amid Boos, Backlash, and BTS Controversy

March 17, 2026 By AsianBus Wire Staff

The 2026 Academy Awards delivered one of its most controversial moments in recent memory when "K-Pop Demon Hunters" took home a major award, only to be met with audible boos from the audience and intense backlash over the acceptance speech.

The film, which blends K-Pop culture with supernatural action, had already divided critics and audiences before Oscar night. But nothing prepared viewers for the chaos that would unfold on Hollywood's biggest stage.

Boos Echo Through Dolby Theatre

When "K-Pop Demon Hunters" was announced as the winner, the reaction from the star-studded audience was immediate and visceral. Boos could be heard throughout the Dolby Theatre, marking a rare moment of open hostility at the typically decorous ceremony.

The film had reportedly suffered what many called "severe disrespect" throughout awards season, with critics questioning its nomination and Hollywood insiders allegedly dismissing its artistic merit.

The Controversial Speech

If the win itself was controversial, the acceptance speech poured gasoline on the fire. The film's creator delivered remarks that immediately sparked backlash across social media, though specific details of what was said have been debated.

Stars from the film have since "savagely hit back" at critics, defending both the movie and the speech. Their responses have further polarized audiences, with some praising their defiance and others criticizing their handling of the situation.

BTS Connection Sparks Additional Controversy

Adding another layer to the drama, cast members made comments about BTS during interviews that "set off" ARMYs worldwide. The exact nature of the BTS claims remains contentious, with fans accusing the stars of inappropriate comparisons or comments about the iconic group.

One actress was "shocked" when a BTS connection emerged during her Oscar interview in what fans are calling a "viral cameo" moment — though the exact context remains a subject of debate.

What This Means for K-Pop in Hollywood

Despite the controversy, "K-Pop Demon Hunters" represents a milestone for Korean entertainment's influence in Hollywood. Whether the negative attention will help or hurt future K-Pop-inspired projects remains to be seen.

"Any publicity is good publicity," argued one industry analyst. "The film is now the most talked-about Oscar winner in years. That has value."

Others disagree, suggesting that the controversy may make Hollywood studios more cautious about similar projects in the future.
