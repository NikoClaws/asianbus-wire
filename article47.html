Seo Kang Joon Confirmed For 'Another Love But You' Opposite Ahn Eun Jin

March 19, 2026 By Grace Kim 📖 7 min read

The Hallyu wave continues to deliver exciting casting news as Seo Kang Joon and Ahn Eun Jin have been confirmed as the lead couple in the upcoming romance drama "Another Love But You." According to Soompi and multiple Korean entertainment outlets, the drama will mark the first on-screen collaboration between these two rising stars, pairing Seo Kang Joon's established heartthrob appeal with Ahn Eun Jin's critically acclaimed acting abilities.

The announcement comes as Seo Kang Joon rides the success of his recent role in "The Boyfriend on Demand," while Ahn Eun Jin continues to build on the momentum from her acclaimed performance in "Dynamite Kiss." Together, they represent a compelling combination of commercial appeal and artistic credibility that has industry observers anticipating strong viewership.

Plot Details

"Another Love But You" explores the complicated terrain of second chances in love. The drama tells the story of two people who each carry the weight of past relationships as they unexpectedly find themselves falling for each other. The title suggests the central conflict: can you fully love someone new when part of your heart still belongs to someone else?

Seo Kang Joon will play Lee Min-ho (working name), a successful architect who has built emotional walls since a devastating breakup years earlier. His character throws himself into work, creating beautiful spaces for others while his own emotional interior remains deliberately empty. When circumstances force him to confront feelings he thought he'd successfully suppressed, he must decide whether the risk of pain is worth the possibility of love.

Ahn Eun Jin takes on the role of Kang Soo-yeon (working name), a talented interior designer whose professional path crosses with Min-ho's. Unlike him, she hasn't closed herself off from love—she's simply been too focused on establishing her career to pursue it. Her openness challenges Min-ho's carefully constructed defenses, while her own journey involves learning that being ready for love and finding the right person aren't always synchronized.

The drama promises to explore realistic relationship dynamics rather than relying on dramatic coincidences or over-the-top scenarios. The production has emphasized that the story will resonate with viewers who have experienced the complexity of adult relationships—the baggage we carry, the fears that hold us back, and the courage required to try again.

"This isn't a story about perfect people finding perfect love. It's about two imperfect people learning that sometimes the best relationships come when you're brave enough to be vulnerable with someone new." — Production team statement

The Leads

Seo Kang Joon: The 31-year-old actor has evolved significantly since his early days as a model and idol group member. His transition to acting has been marked by increasingly mature role choices, from the youthful romance of "Cheese in the Trap" to more complex characters in recent projects. In "The Boyfriend on Demand," he demonstrated his ability to carry a drama as the sole male lead while bringing emotional depth to romantic scenes.

For "Another Love But You," Seo Kang Joon will reportedly undergo something of a visual transformation, adopting a more mature, worn appearance that reflects his character's emotional state. The actor has spoken about embracing roles that challenge him, and this character's journey from emotional closure to vulnerability appears to offer that challenge.

Ahn Eun Jin: The 33-year-old actress has built an impressive resume across both television and film. Her breakthrough came with "Hospital Playlist," where she won hearts as a complex, layered character. Her recent work in "Dynamite Kiss" earned her multiple award nominations and demonstrated her range in both romantic and dramatic scenes.

Industry observers have noted that Ahn Eun Jin brings a naturalistic quality to her performances—she doesn't feel like she's acting, but rather genuinely experiencing her character's emotions. This quality should serve the realistic tone of "Another Love But You" well.

Chemistry Potential

While the leads have never worked together before, early table reads have reportedly generated excitement among the production team. Both actors are known for their ability to create chemistry with co-stars, and initial reports suggest their pairing feels natural.

The height difference between them (Seo Kang Joon at 183cm and Ahn Eun Jin at 166cm) creates visually appealing framing, while their contrasting acting styles—his more internalized approach versus her more expressive technique—could create interesting dynamic tension on screen.

Fan communities have already begun creating edit videos and concept art imagining their interactions, suggesting strong pre-existing interest in the pairing despite no prior collaborative history.

Production Timeline

Pre-production for "Another Love But You" is currently underway, with script reading sessions ongoing and location scouting in progress. Principal photography is scheduled to begin in early May 2026, with the production aiming for a fall 2026 premiere.

The drama is being produced by Story J Company, known for quality romantic productions. The writer, whose previous works include successful romance dramas, has reportedly been developing the script for over a year, taking time to craft dialogue that feels authentic to adult relationship dynamics.

Broadcasting rights are still being negotiated, with multiple networks and streaming platforms reportedly interested. An announcement regarding the airing platform is expected in the coming weeks.

Supporting Cast Announcements

While the leads have been confirmed, supporting cast announcements are expected throughout April. Rumors suggest several notable names are in negotiations for secondary roles, though no confirmations have been made. The drama is expected to feature a relatively small but significant supporting cast, keeping focus on the central couple's relationship.

Industry Context

The romance drama genre remains hugely popular both domestically and internationally, with Korean productions dominating streaming charts worldwide. "Another Love But You" enters a competitive landscape that includes several high-profile romance dramas scheduled for late 2026.

However, the combination of established stars, realistic storytelling, and quality production pedigree positions the drama favorably. If the execution matches the promise, it could become one of the year's most talked-about romance dramas.

For Seo Kang Joon, success here would further cement his position as a leading man capable of carrying prestige projects. For Ahn Eun Jin, it represents an opportunity to expand her commercial appeal while maintaining the artistic credibility she's carefully built.

What To Expect

While much remains to be revealed about "Another Love But You," the elements in place suggest a thoughtful, emotionally resonant drama aimed at viewers who appreciate nuanced storytelling over dramatic pyrotechnics. The emphasis on realistic adult relationships, combined with the talents of its leads, promises a viewing experience that balances entertainment with emotional authenticity.

Fans of either actor have reason for excitement, while romance drama enthusiasts have another promising project to add to their watchlist. As production progresses, more details will emerge about the specific story, additional casting, and premiere timing.
