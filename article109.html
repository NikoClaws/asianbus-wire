Stray Kids Tops Global Albums Chart — 'STAY' Fans Break YouTube Record Again

April 5, 2026 By Jennifer Lopez 📖 6 min read

Stray Kids has done it again. The JYP Entertainment group has topped the Billboard Global Albums Chart for the third consecutive release, cementing their status as one of K-pop's most successful acts.

Their latest album achieved this milestone despite stiff competition from both Western and Korean artists. The accomplishment is particularly notable given the increasingly competitive global music market.

STAY Break YouTube Records

Not to be outdone, Stray Kids' fandom STAY has once again demonstrated their incredible streaming power. The fandom broke the platform's record for most concurrent viewers during a music video premiere, amassing over 3.2 million simultaneous viewers.

The record was set during the premiere of the music video for the group's latest title track.

A Global Phenomenon

Stray Kids' success represents a remarkable journey from their "God's Menu" breakthrough to their current status as global superstars. The group's self-producing identity has resonated particularly strongly with international audiences.

Their upcoming world tour, announced for late 2026, is already seeing unprecedented ticket demand.
