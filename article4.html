ILLIT Under Fire: Double Plagiarism Accusations From BLACKPINK and NewJeans Fans

March 17, 2026By AsianBus Wire Staff

HYBE's rookie girl group ILLIT finds themselves in hot water as fans of both BLACKPINK and NewJeans accuse them of plagiarizing their favorite artists.

The controversy erupted when eagle-eyed BLINKs noticed striking similarities between ILLIT's latest album concept and BLACKPINK's iconic styling. Meanwhile, NewJeans fans have compiled evidence of alleged choreography copying.

The BLACKPINK Accusations

BLINKS have created detailed comparison posts showing alleged similarities in styling, concept photos, and even music video aesthetics between ILLIT and BLACKPINK's earlier work.

The NewJeans Accusations

Adding to the controversy, Bunnies (NewJeans' fanbase) have pointed out what they claim are copied dance moves and formations in ILLIT's recent performances. Video comparisons have gone viral on social media.

HYBE's Complicated Position

The situation is particularly awkward for HYBE, as NewJeans is also under their corporate umbrella (through sublabel ADOR). Industry observers note this adds another layer to the ongoing tensions within HYBE's multi-label structure.
