BTS Takes 6th Music Show Win For 'SWIM' On M Countdown — Dominance Ahead Of ARIRANG World Tour

April 2, 2026 By Min-ji Park 📖 6 min read

BTS has claimed their sixth music show trophy for "SWIM," continuing their dominant comeback run on the April 2 episode of Mnet's M Countdown.

The final showdown came down to BTS's "SWIM" versus ITZY member Yuna's solo debut track "Ice Cream." BTS took the crown with an overwhelming 10,150 points, cementing the track's status as the biggest hit of the spring season.

"SWIM" was released on March 20 as the lead single from BTS's sixth Korean-language studio album Arirang, which marked the group's long-awaited comeback after a four-year hiatus during which all seven members completed their mandatory military service.

Stacked Performances On Today's Show

The April 2 episode featured a stacked lineup of performers alongside BTS's win. ITZY's Yuna performed "Ice Cream" from her highly anticipated solo debut, while Red Velvet's Irene showcased her solo track "Biggest Fan."

Other highlights included Kep1er performing "KILLA (Face the other me)," PENTAGON's Kino with "TAXI," and VERIVERY's Kangmin with "Free Falling." The show was MCed by TREASURE's So Jung Hwan, ZEROBASEONE's Park Gun Wook, and KickFlip's Kyehoon.

Additional performers on today's episode included Jang Haneum, Baby DONT Cry, CSR, AtHeart, ALL(H)OURS, SLAY, AmbiO, S2IT, V01D, and Seo EVE.

MAMAMOO Also Making Headlines

In other tour news, MAMAMOO announced dates and locations for their 2026 world tour today. The group will perform across 13 stops including Seoul, Kaohsiung, Macau, Singapore, Manila, New York, Chicago, Fort Worth, Cedar Park, Los Angeles, San Jose, Tacoma, and Hong Kong.

With BTS's ARIRANG tour launching next week, TXT's comeback on April 13, and now MAMAMOO joining the touring circuit, April is shaping up to be one of the busiest months in K-pop history.
