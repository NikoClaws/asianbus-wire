Ha Hyun Sang Drops Second Studio Album 'New Boat'

The blues rock virtuoso returns with 12 tracks exploring love, loss, and redemption

By Yuki Tanaka • April 4, 2026
5 min read

Ha Hyun Sang has returned with his most ambitious project yet. The Grammy-nominated blues rock artist released his second full-length studio album "New Boat" on April 4, featuring 12 tracks that showcase his evolution as both a songwriter and musician.

The album arrives four years after his acclaimed debut and represents a significant artistic leap forward. Known for his powerful vocals and emotionally charged lyrics, Ha Hyun Sang explores new sonic territory while maintaining the raw authenticity that earned him international recognition.

A Labor of Love

The album was recorded over 18 months, with Ha Hyun Sang involved in every aspect of production. "I wanted to create something that felt true to my journey," he explained during an interview. "Each song is a piece of my life from the past few years."

The title track "New Boat" has been particularly praised by critics for its sweeping arrangement and introspective lyrics. The music video, shot in black and white, has resonated with viewers for its emotional simplicity.

International Recognition

Ha Hyun Sang gained widespread attention after his Grammy nomination for his work on the "KPop Demon Hunters" soundtrack. He was recently featured in Rolling Stone's "Artists to Watch" list for 2026.

The album is available on all streaming platforms, with a physical edition set for release later this month.

About Ha Hyun Sang

Ha Hyun Sang is a South Korean blues rock artist known for his powerful vocals and Grammy-nominated songwriting. He gained international fame through "KPop Demon Hunters" soundtrack contributions.
